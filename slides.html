Who is the customer?



People who have a blog or a website.

What are we doing?



We are building a platform that serves

real-time social games and apps

to bloggers and website owners.

No server side installation is necessary.

What will a blogger get?


  • Rylyz Widget: displays a game console
  • Apps: engages visitors, brings them back
  • Promotion: players from other sites will visit
  • Profit sharing: from virtual goods and subscriptions
